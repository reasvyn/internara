## 🔑 Maintainership Policy

Maintainers are selected based on their consistent high-quality contributions, deep technical
understanding of the system's modular architecture, and commitment to the **3S Doctrine**.

### Responsibilities:

- Reviewing pull requests for architectural compliance.
- Triage of issues and bugs in their domain modules.
- Enforcing the project's documentation and testing standards.
- Mentoring new contributors to the Internara ecosystem.
