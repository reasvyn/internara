# Chapter 15: Internship Registration & Placement

> **Last updated:** 2026-06-27 **Changes:** initial content — add registration, placement, and
> account application workflow

## Description

This chapter covers how students register for internship programs, how administrators place students
at partner companies, and how students apply for company accounts.

---

## 15.1 Student Registration

Students browse published internship programs and submit registration requests.

### 15.1.1 Browsing Available Internships

1. Navigate to **Student Portal → Internships** from the sidebar
2. Browse the list of published internship programs
3. Click on a program to view details: dates, phases, grading weights, and required documents

### 15.1.2 Submitting a Registration

1. Open the internship program you want to join
2. Click **Register**
3. The system checks eligibility:
    - You must have an active academic year
    - You must not already be registered for the same period
4. Submit your registration request

Your registration appears as **Pending** until an administrator reviews it.

### 15.1.3 Registration Statuses

| Status        | Description                                |
| ------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| **Pending**   | Awaiting administrator review              |
| **Approved**  | Registration accepted, awaiting placement  |
| **Rejected**  | Registration declined (reason provided)    |
| **Cancelled** | Registration withdrawn by student or admin |

---

## 15.2 Administrator Review

Administrators review pending registrations and decide whether to approve or reject them.

### 15.2.1 Viewing Pending Registrations

1. Navigate to **Admin → Registrations** from the sidebar
2. The table shows all registrations with pending status
3. Filter by internship program, student name, or date

### 15.2.2 Approving a Registration

1. Click **Review** on a pending registration
2. Review the student's academic year and eligibility
3. Click **Approve** to accept the registration
4. The student is notified of the approval

### 15.2.3 Rejecting a Registration

1. Click **Review** on a pending registration
2. Select a rejection reason or type a custom message
3. Click **Reject**
4. The student is notified with the reason

---

## 15.3 Student Placement

After a registration is approved, the student needs to be placed at a partner company.

### 15.3.1 Available Placement Slots

Each company partnership defines a number of available slots per internship period. A slot becomes
available when:

- The company partnership is active
- The slot count has not been reached
- The internship period matches the program dates

### 15.3.2 Assigning a Student to a Company

1. Navigate to **Admin → Placements** from the sidebar
2. Select a student with an approved registration
3. Choose a partner company from the list
4. Select the placement slot (if the company has multiple)
5. Confirm the placement

The student is notified of their placement and can view their assigned company in the student
portal.

### 15.3.3 Placement Statuses

| Status         | Description                                     |
| -------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| **Active**     | Student is placed and the internship is ongoing |
| **Completed**  | Student has finished the internship period      |
| **Terminated** | Placement ended early (reason recorded)         |

### 15.3.4 Reassigning a Placement

If a placement needs to change (company cancellation, student request):

1. Navigate to **Admin → Placements**
2. Find the active placement
3. Click **Reassign**
4. Select a new company and slot
5. Confirm the change

The previous placement is recorded as **Terminated** and the new one becomes **Active**.

---

## 15.4 Account Applications

Some companies require students to apply for a platform account before starting their internship.

### 15.4.1 Applying for a Company Account

1. From the student portal, navigate to **Placements → My Placement**
2. Click **Apply for Account**
3. Fill in any required information requested by the company
4. Submit the application

### 15.4.2 Approving Account Applications

Company supervisors or administrators review and approve account applications:

1. Navigate to **Supervisor Portal → Account Applications**
2. Review the application details
3. Click **Approve** or **Reject**
4. If approved, the student gains access to the company's platform features

---

## 15.5 Change Requests

Students or administrators can request changes to an active placement.

### 15.5.1 Requesting a Change

1. Navigate to **Placements → My Placement**
2. Click **Request Change**
3. Select the type of change:
    - **Company transfer** — move to a different company
    - **Schedule adjustment** — modify start/end dates
    - **Withdrawal** — leave the program entirely
4. Provide a reason
5. Submit the request

### 15.5.2 Reviewing Change Requests

Administrators review change requests and approve or reject them:

1. Navigate to **Admin → Change Requests**
2. Review the request details and reason
3. Approve or reject with a response message
